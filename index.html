Why Race?

I really liked swimming when I was younger and even competed. In highschool and unversity I started running and really enjoyed being outside and getting a runner's high and feeling like I could run for miles. I didn't start cycling until I moved to London mostly so I wouldn't have to pay for public transport. But after going out with friends and being able to see the countryside I really liked cycling. Finally it just made sense to put them all together.
